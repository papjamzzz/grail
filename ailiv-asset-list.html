Signal Bridge
LIVE
ailiv.health/signal-bridge
Cross-platform correlation tool. Links Apple Health and WHOOP data across 4 time periods. Surfaces relationships between metrics that wouldn't be visible in either app alone.
Flask route: /signal-bridge · Apple Health + WHOOP data correlation
4 time windows: Morning · Afternoon · Evening · Sleep · Chord diagram visualization
AI INTEGRATED · LIVE
AI button per time window → POST /api/bridge-analysis → 5i synthesis (Claude + GPT + Gemini). Sends active Apple×WHOOP correlation pairs with strength scores for the selected time window.

ailiv.health/rootcause
Force-directed network map. Connects flagged labs to root causes to targeted protocols. Shows why something is off and what to do about it — not just what the number is.
Flask route: /rootcause · Force-directed graph (D3.js)

AI INTEGRATED · LIVE
AI button → POST /api/roots-analysis → 5i synthesis (Claude + GPT + Gemini). Sends all flagged lab nodes, root cause nodes, and intervention nodes. Returns top 3 highest-leverage protocol priorities with timelines.
