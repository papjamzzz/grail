How to read your Atlas
Every day, your body tells one connected story. Here’s the key.
The center is today’s headline system. Everything around it either lifts it up or pulls it down.
Orbiting orbs are the drivers — sleep, HRV, strain, energy. Hover any orb for what it means today.
Solid line — this driver is supporting the center. A steady pulse flows inward.
Fine dashed line — this driver is draining the center. Its pulse flows outward, away.
Wide dashed line — neutral context. Related, but not helping or hurting right now.
Travelling dots are live signal moving between systems — the atlas is breathing, not a snapshot.
