User Guide · Bloody Roots
Bloody Roots
A living map of your biological risk chains — from flagged labs to root drivers to targeted interventions.
The Three Layers
Every node belongs to one of three layers, arranged vertically from bottom to top.
Flagged Labs
Biomarkers currently outside their optimal range. The starting point of every chain. Each orb shows your current value and what's driving it.
Root Causes
The upstream physiological mechanisms driving your flagged markers — HPA dysregulation, mitochondrial stress, chronic inflammation, and more.
Interventions
Evidence-based protocols that target the root causes — with specific dosing, timing, and clinical rationale. Red badge = high urgency.
Interacting
click node
Illuminates the full chain — all connected nodes highlight in green, everything else fades. A detail panel slides in on the right.
click background
Resets all highlights and closes the detail panel.
drag node
Repositions any node. Physics simulation keeps the network coherent. Release to let it settle.
scroll
Zooms the canvas in and out. Pinch to zoom on trackpad.
hover node
Shows a quick tooltip with the node name, type, and current lab value (for flagged labs).
The Detail Panel
When you click a node, the panel on the right shows all connected nodes as chips — click any chip to jump to that node's chain. The note below explains the clinical significance, dosing, or mechanism in plain language.
Dark Mode
Toggle between light and dark using the switch in the top-right corner. The network adapts fully — all SVG elements recolor on switch.
